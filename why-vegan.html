If We Can, Why Not?

Let's reduce unnecessary suffering. If we can enjoy food that's tasty and healthy without cruelty, then why not?

As someone who fosters cats and helps stray kittens find a new home, I could not imagine myself eating a kitten just because it tastes 'delicious'. For me, a brief fleeting moment of pleasure does not justify causing harm or suffering to another sentient being. The same logic applies to the billions of animals raised and slaughtered for food each year.

Modern food science and culinary creativity mean that plant-based alternatives are better than ever. From creamy oat milk to incredible meat substitutes like Juicy Marbles, there's never been a better time to explore compassionate eating.
