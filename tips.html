Tips & Tricks

How to make your Veganuary a success

Setting You Up for Success

I've completed Veganuary twice â€” once with friends, and once on my own. Both times were rewarding! Here are my tips for making it easier and more enjoyable.

1 Do It With Friends

It was definitely more fun with friends! Find a Veganuary buddy to share recipes, celebrate wins, and keep each other motivated. You can compare notes on what worked and what didn't.

2 Eat Enough (Seriously!)

One of the biggest mistakes people make is not eating enough. Plant-based foods are often less calorie-dense, so you might need larger portions. Don't go hungry â€” load up on protein-rich foods like tofu, tempeh, lentils, chickpeas, and quinoa.

3 Identify plant-based substitutes that work for you

What was helpful for me was finding plant-based substitutes that mimicked the taste and texture of familiar animal products. For instance, nutritional yeast (parmesan substitute), oat milk (creamy milk substitute), and tofu (versatile protein source). White pepper and tofu skin is also a close substitute for chicken. Experiment with different brands and types to find what you like best.

4 Plan Your Meals

Planning your meals ahead of time can save you from last-minute stress and unhealthy choices. I find myself reaching for instant ramen less often when I have a plan, especially when you are faced with a smaller range of familiar ingredients. Create a weekly menu, shop accordingly, and prep ingredients in advance to make cooking easier and more enjoyable.
